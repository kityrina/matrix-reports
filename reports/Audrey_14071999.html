Heart (Anahata) · 3 — The Empress

Body systems: Heart, circulatory system, respiratory system, lungs, bronchi, thoracic spine, ribs, shoulder scapular area, chest.

The 3 (The Empress) rules your heart chakra — literal heart, lungs, circulatory system, breasts. This is your abundance centre, and it's strong when you're in creative flow and nurturing what matters. The danger is over-giving until your chest tightens. Watch for shallow breathing when you're stressed, heart palpitations during emotional overload, and lung issues when you've been mothering everyone but yourself. The Empress needs to receive as much as she gives. If you're always the one pouring out, the heart closes to protect itself. Breathwork, letting others care for you, and saying no without guilt keep this centre open. Your body will remind you when the give-take ratio is off.

Solar Plexus (Manipura) · 8 — Justice

Digestive issues here are not random — they flare when you're out of integrity or swallowing injustice. If your gut is a mess, ask what truth you're refusing to act on. This centre heals through radical honesty, clear boundaries, and keeping your word to yourself first. The scales must balance, and your body enforces it when you won't.

Sacral (Svadhisthana) · 18 — The Moon

This arcanum also marks psychic permeability — you absorb others' emotions through the sacral if your boundaries are weak.
