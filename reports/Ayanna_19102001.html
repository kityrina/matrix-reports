New Strategy

Channel the 15's raw power into *constructive* change — which means staying in the structure long enough to reform it from the inside. 2. Find one container and stay in it for six months, no matter what. A job. A course. A relationship. A creative project. Choose something that matters and make a vow: you don't leave until the six months are up, even if it gets uncomfortable. Practice negotiating *inside* the structure instead of nuking it. 3. Work with a somatic practitioner on the rage-to-flight pattern. Your body holds the rebellion as a survival response. You need someone trained in trauma release (somatic experiencing, TRE, or EMDR) to help you discharge the old fight without directing it at your current life. The goal: feel the fire, don't become it.
