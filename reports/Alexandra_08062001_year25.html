The 15 dominates your day-to-day—it's louder, hotter, harder to ignore. The 6 surfaces in the pause afterward, when the noise clears and you see what you actually picked. That's when the undercurrent shows its hand. You'll know the 6 is speaking when the question isn't *can I have this?* but *do I want to live with choosing this?*
