  • Minimising compliments or help offered
  • Feeling like you did it all yourself

2. The Three-Before-Me Rule: In any meeting, conversation, or group setting, let three people speak or contribute before you offer your take. 3. Monthly Reverse Mentorship: Identify one person — younger, less experienced, or outside your field — and ask them to teach you something they know that you don't. The 5 (Hierophant) karmic lesson is that hierarchy isn't fixed. Let someone else be the expert. Your job is to be the eager student. Do this once a month. Watch your empire grow.
