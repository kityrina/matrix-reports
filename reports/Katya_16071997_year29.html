The Chariot wants velocity; the Lovers wants alignment. Expect at least one moment this year where you have to pick: keep going at full speed, or pause and honour what your heart actually needs.

The Chariot dominates your visible life — the work, the wins, the forward march. The Lovers surfaces in private moments, late-night conversations, and every time you feel the gap between what you're achieving and whether it's feeding your soul. Don't ignore that gap. The year works when you let both speak.
