  • Difficulty receiving help from others

New Strategy

Let people be your equals — not your projects. The new pattern starts with your 6 (The Lovers) centre and purpose: you're here to *partner*, not oversee. 1. Practice receiving without reciprocating immediately. When someone offers help, advice, or a favour, say yes and sit in the discomfort of not balancing the scales that week. 2. Implement a 60-second pause before offering solutions. When someone shares a problem, count to sixty before speaking. If they want your input, they'll ask. If they don't, you've just practiced witnessing without managing. This teaches your Hermit that presence — not expertise — is the real gift. Track it in your phone notes: how many times did they solve it themselves?

3. Delegate one entire project this month and do not supervise. Hand it to someone capable, set clear outcomes, then step back. No check-ins unless they initiate. No "just tweaking this real quick." Let them own it — including the mess. Your World energy will panic. Let it. You're learning that completion doesn't require your fingerprints on every surface.
