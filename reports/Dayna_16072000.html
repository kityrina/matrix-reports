New Strategy

You don't need to kill the warrior — you need to redirect her. The new strategy is contained momentum: choose your battles, commit fully when you do, and finish what you start. 2. **Pre-Commitment Ritual.** Before any decision that matters (saying yes to an opportunity, having a hard conversation, making a move), write down: *What am I protecting by doing this?* Then commit out loud — to a person, a mirror, a voice note. The 12 taught you to sacrifice yourself; this flips it. You're sacrificing hesitation for something you're guarding. Externalizing the commitment breaks the inner suspension.

3. **Reclaim the Charge.** Weekly: pick one micro-battle and see it through to the end without apologizing. A boundary with a friend. A negotiation. Sending the thing you've been sitting on. The goal isn't to win — it's to complete the cycle from impulse to action to resolution. Your nervous system needs proof that moving forward doesn't destroy anyone. Start small, build evidence, let the handbrake release.
