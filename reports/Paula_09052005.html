3. Assign yourself one area of life where you deliberately let someone else lead — a project at work, a weekend plan, a decision about money or logistics. Your only job: follow without commentary. Another 6 here — your willpower and confidence live or die by alignment with your values. Digestive issues, stomach knots, or that sinking feeling in your gut? You're in a situation that violates what you actually want. The solar plexus 6 means your sense of self-worth is tied to whether you're honoring your own choices or bending to someone else's script. This is also your centre destiny, so your entire life purpose pulses through this chakra. When you choose from the heart (even if it's the harder path), your digestion runs clean and your energy stays high. When you people-please, your gut shuts down. The fix is always the same: come back to what YOU want, not what keeps everyone comfortable.

Sacral (Svadhisthana) · 13 — Death (Rebirth)

Body systems: Adrenal glands, uterus and ovaries, kidneys, intestines, prostate gland in men, lumbar spinal column.

Death (13) sits here, and this is the transformation engine — your sexuality, creativity, and reproductive system are all wired for radical reinvention. This chakra doesn't malfunction; it *demolishes* what's stagnant. If you're clinging to a creative project, a relationship, or an identity past its expiration date, the sacral will force the ending through menstrual chaos, libido crashes, or lower-back pain that won't quit. The 13 asks you to let things die on time. Your creative and sexual energy is most alive when you're willing to become someone new. Don't drag out the old skin. Regular pelvic release (movement, massage, or letting yourself actually feel your emotions instead of white-knuckling through them) keeps this channel flowing. The sacral 13 also connects to your money path — your financial rebirths will ask you to let go before you leap.

Root (Muladhara) · 7 — The Chariot

Body systems: Urogenital system, lower limbs, large intestine, tailbone, sacrum, legs.

The 7 (The Chariot) here means your survival instinct runs on forward momentum. Legs, feet, bones, adrenals — they thrive when you're actively building something, expanding, moving your life in a clear direction. Stagnation makes you sick. If you're stuck in a job or city or relationship that's going nowhere, your root chakra will start to ache: knee problems, lower-body injuries, chronic fatigue, adrenal burnout. The 7 also governs your money channel, so financial stress lands in your legs and bones. What clears it: literal movement (walking, running, anything that reminds your body it can GO), and making one tangible decision that pushes your life forward. You're not built to stay still. Give the Chariot a road.
