Example: "I need you to follow through when you say you'll call" instead of the usual cold withdrawal when they don't. Notice the discomfort. Notice whether the relationship survives. (It will. 3. Weekly Inventory: The Mask Audit. Every Sunday, journal three moments from the week where you performed instead of showed up. What did you hide? Who did you charm instead of challenge?
