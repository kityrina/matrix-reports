3. Practice finishing *small* things as training wheels — a book, a short course, a 30-day habit, a difficult conversation that needs three follow-ups instead of one dramatic exit. Prove to yourself that completion doesn't kill you. The Destroyer strategy assumes staying = death. Disprove it with evidence. One small finish at a time.
