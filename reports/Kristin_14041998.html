1. **Weekly "Knowledge Out" Practice**: Every Sunday, post or send one thing you've learned that week — a voice note to a friend, a single-paragraph insight on a private account, a tip shared in a Slack channel. 2. **Somatic Check-In Before Retreat**: When the urge to disappear hits — inbox overwhelm, a difficult conversation, someone asking what you do — pause and do a 60-second body scan. Where is the contraction? Throat, chest, belly? Name it out loud: "I want to ghost because I'm scared of being misunderstood." Then ask: is withdrawal actually safer right now, or just familiar? 3. **One Collaborative Anchor Per Quarter**: Pick one project, client, or creative partnership where you agree to stay visible throughout — no mid-process vanishing. Let someone else see your drafts, your pivots, your "I don't know yet" moments. This isn't about being extroverted; it's about proving that your magic survives contact with other humans.
