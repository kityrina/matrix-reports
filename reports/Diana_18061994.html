Love and money are wired together for you — the 6 means partnership is the portal through which financial abundance flows. You'll feel it as resentment, dead bedroom, and ironically — money drying up anyway. The fusion works when love is chosen cleanly and money follows as a byproduct of that alignment. It breaks when you try to optimize the choice or stay because leaving feels financially reckless. Diana, your most profitable relationships will be the ones where desire came first and the empire you built together came second — never the reverse.
