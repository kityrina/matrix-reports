Stay in the room when it gets uncomfortable. 2. Weekly audit: "What did I withhold this week that would have served the situation?" Not secrets — just insight, preference, or honesty you kept to yourself out of fear it would be misused. Write it down. Notice the pattern. You'll see most of it was safe to share and you invented the danger.

3. Somatic grounding when the hermit urge spikes. The 18-9-9 tail lives in your nervous system as a freeze response dressed up as wisdom.
