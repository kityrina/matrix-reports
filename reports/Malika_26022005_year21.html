Arcanum 1

Okay, Malika — the year ahead at age 21 is governed by the 15 (The Devil / Passion), which means this is not a year for playing it safe. This year doesn't ask you to behave — it asks you to integrate your shadow and walk forward with eyes wide open.

This year tests whether you can hold freedom AND accountability at the same time, whether you can want something and not bolt the second it asks you to grow up. The cause is old refusal. The cure is staying.
